## Stats

### Primary Attributes
| Stat | Full Name      | Category Mapping              |
|------|----------------|------------------------------|
| STR  | Strength       | Exercise, physical tasks      |
| DEX  | Dexterity      | Quick tasks, errands, travel |
| CON  | Constitution   | Long-duration tasks          |
| INT  | Intelligence   | Coding, technical work      |
| WIS  | Wisdom         | Research, learning, reading  |
| CHA  | Charisma       | Meetings, social tasks       |

### Stat Progression
- Base stats: 10 for all attributes
- +1 to relevant stat every 5 completions in that category
- +2 stat points per level after Level 3 (player choice)
- Maximum stat: 20 (natural) + 5 (class bonuses)

### Stat Effects
- **STR**: Affects physical quest difficulty reduction
- **DEX**: Reduces time needed for quick tasks
- **CON**: Provides bonus XP for long-duration quests
- **INT**: Increases coding quest XP multiplier
- **WIS**: Higher chance of "epic" research discoveries
- **CHA**: Better outcomes in meeting quests

## Boss Fight Mechanics

### Boss Types
- **The Dragon of Deadlines**: Time-sensitive major projects
- **The Lich of Legacy Code**: Complex refactoring tasks
- **The Behemoth of Bureaucracy**: Administrative or compliance tasks
- **The Phoenix of Presentations**: High-stakes public speaking

### Encounter Structure
1. **Phase 1: The Approach** (25% completion)
   - Setup narration
   - Initial challenge description
   - Simulated d20 roll for flavor
   
2. **Phase 2: The Twist** (50% completion)
   - Complication or new obstacle
   - Mid-battle challenge
   - Environmental effect
   
3. **Phase 3: The Resolution** (100% completion)
   - Final confrontation
   - Victory conditions
   - Epic conclusion

### Dice Mechanics
- Simulated d20 rolls for narrative flavor only
- Real-world task completion = automatic victory
- Roll outcomes affect narration, not success/failure
- Critical hits (20) and misses (1) create special narration

## Game Balance Notes

### Difficulty Scaling
- Quest difficulty increases with player level
- XP rewards scale to maintain progression pace
- Boss fights become more complex at higher levels
- Stat requirements for certain quests

### Anti-Exploitation
- Daily quest completion limits
- Streak reset after missed day
- Diminishing returns on repeat quest types
- Minimum time requirements between quest completions

### Player Retention
- Daily login bonuses
- Weekly challenges
- Long-term progression goals
- Social features and leaderboards
